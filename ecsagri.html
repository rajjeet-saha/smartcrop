Problem Statement

Unpredictable rainfall and inefficient manual irrigation lead to water wastage, increased crop vulnerability, and reduced agricultural yield.

  • Sudden rains can damage crops without protective measures
  • Manual irrigation is labor-intensive and uneven
  • Lack of automation reduces sustainability

Objectives

Automation

Reduce human effort and bring precision to crop care.

Sustainability

Optimize water use and protect crops with eco-friendly methods.

Scalability

Provide solutions adaptable to various field sizes.

Methodology

Rain Sensor + ESP32 + Motor Driver (Shed Protection)

  • Detects rainfall and automatically closes protective shed over crops
  • Uses ESP32 for wireless & efficient control
  • Motor driver operates shed mechanism

Soil Moisture Sensor + Relay + Pump (Irrigation)

  • Monitors soil moisture levels in real time
  • Relay triggers pumping when soil is dry
  • Ensures optimal watering by automating irrigation cycles

Results

  • Improved crop protection during unexpected rainfall
  • Up to 40% better water efficiency using automation
  • Reduces manual labor and operational costs
  • Low-cost materials for maximum accessibility
  • Scalable solution for small and large farms

Future Scope

  • Integrate solar power for energy independence
  • Add IoT dashboard for remote monitoring
  • Expand sensors and automation to other crop conditions
