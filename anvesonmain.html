Smart Automatic Crop Protection and Irrigation System

Revolutionizing agriculture with eco-tech automation for sustainable farming.

Problem Statement

Rain and Irrigation Challenges

Traditional farming faces issues like excessive rain damaging crops and inefficient manual irrigation leading to water wastage and inconsistent yields.

Objectives

Automation

Automate crop protection and irrigation to reduce manual labor.

Sustainability

Promote eco-friendly practices for long-term agricultural health.

Efficiency

Optimize resource use for better crop yields and cost savings.

Methodology

Crop Protection (Rain Sensor)

Uses a rain sensor connected to ESP32 and motor driver to automatically deploy a shed during rain.

  • Rain Sensor: Detects precipitation.
  • ESP32: Microcontroller for processing.
  • Motor Driver: Controls shed mechanism.

Irrigation (Soil Sensor)

Soil sensor with relay and pump ensures optimal watering based on moisture levels.

  • Soil Sensor: Measures soil moisture.
  • Relay: Switches pump on/off.
  • Pump: Delivers water as needed.
